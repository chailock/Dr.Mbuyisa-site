About Dr Mbuyisa

Dr Mbuyisa is a highly respected and fully qualified traditional healer (sangoma and inyanga) proudly serving the community of Volksrust, Mpumalanga, and surrounding areas.

Called by the ancestors from a young age, Dr Mbuyisa underwent the rigorous initiation process known as ukuthwasa under the guidance of experienced mentors. This multi-year training included deep spiritual development, mastery of divination techniques (such as throwing bones/ukubhula), extensive knowledge of indigenous herbs and muti preparation, ancestral communication, and rituals for cleansing, protection, and harmony.

With over 15 years of dedicated practice, Dr Mbuyisa is committed to restoring balance in body, mind, and spirit. Services are delivered with utmost confidentiality, cultural respect, and compassion.

Qualifications & Training

  • Ancestral calling (ukubizwa ngamadlozi) and completed ukuthwasa initiation
  • Advanced training in herbal medicine and traditional muti formulation
  • Expert in bone throwing (ukubhula) and other divination methods
  • Specialized in spiritual cleansing, protection rituals, and ancestral veneration
  • Affiliated with recognized traditional healer organizations in Mpumalanga
  • Over 15 years of continuous practice and community service
